About The Night House

The Night House earns its name after dark — a private bubble dome with a queen bed, where the Milky Way is your ceiling.

Below it sits a modern desert home with a saltwater pool, jacuzzi, pickleball and bocce courts, and a firepit built for long desert nights. Two king bedrooms inside, room for the whole crew, and some of the darkest skies in California overhead.
