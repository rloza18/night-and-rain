Tucked into the quiet edge of Yucca Valley, The Outpost is a tiny home that earns its name — remote enough to feel like an escape, appointed enough to feel like a splurge.

Step outside to your own saltwater pool, steaming hot tub, cedar sauna, and a dusty mini-golf green that rivals any in the county. Nights end around the firepit under an impossibly wide sky. Come in, cowboy.
