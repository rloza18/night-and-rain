Explore Joshua Tree

Your basecamp for
the high desert.

From the national park's boulders to neon honky-tonks and the darkest skies in California — it's all minutes from your door.

Why here

There's a reason people keep coming back.

Joshua Tree is equal parts adventure and stillness — world-class hiking and climbing by day, otherworldly quiet and star-filled skies by night. Stay with Night & Rain and you're right in the middle of all of it.

The Park

Joshua Tree National Park

Nearly 800,000 acres of boulders, twisting trees, and desert horizon — and the gate is a short drive from every one of our homes.

  • Hidden Valley Nature Trail — an easy, iconic loop through the boulders
  • Keys View — sweeping views to the Coachella Valley and San Andreas Fault
  • Cholla Cactus Garden — best at sunrise or sunset
  • Barker Dam & Skull Rock — short, classic walks

After Dark

Stargazing & the Night Sky

The high desert has some of the darkest, clearest skies in California. On a moonless night the Milky Way is impossible to miss — no telescope required.

  • Fall asleep under the stars in The Night House's bubble dome
  • Stargaze straight from your own firepit or pool
  • Time your trip to a meteor shower for the full show

Eat & Drink

Food & Drink

The desert food scene punches well above its weight — from legendary roadhouses to third-wave coffee.

  • Pappy & Harriet's — Pioneertown's famous BBQ & live-music roadhouse
  • Joshua Tree Coffee Company — the local morning ritual
  • Crossroads Café & La Copine — well-loved desert favorites
  • …plus the spots we send every guest — finalized from our guidebooks

Culture

Art & Desert Culture

Joshua Tree has drawn artists and dreamers for decades — and it shows everywhere you look.

  • Noah Purifoy Outdoor Desert Art Museum — sprawling assemblage sculpture
  • The Integratron — the famous sound bath in nearby Landers
  • Pioneertown — a 1940s Old West film set you can still wander
  • Galleries and shops throughout Joshua Tree village

Get Out There

Adventures & Day Trips

When you want to roam beyond the yard, there's no shortage of ways to fill a day.

  • World-class rock climbing & bouldering
  • Guided off-road and Jeep tours of the backcountry
  • Desert hot springs for a slow afternoon
  • Under an hour to Palm Springs & the Aerial Tramway
