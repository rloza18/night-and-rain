About The Dojo

The Dojo doesn't take itself too seriously — and that's exactly the point. A bold anime mural commands the exterior, shelves of collectibles line the walls inside, and a full gaming setup means the fun doesn't stop when the sun goes down.

And yet: same serene Yucca Valley setting as always. Private saltwater pool, hot tub, sauna, mini-golf, and Joshua trees just outside the fence. Earn your rest. Then geek out.
