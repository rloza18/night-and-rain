Tucked into the quiet edge of Yucca Valley, fifteen minutes from the national park, The Night House is a pint-sized desert hideaway that lives far larger than its footprint.

Step outside to a saltwater pool, a steaming hot tub, a cedar sauna, and a mini-golf green — all yours, all framed by Joshua trees and open sky. Days wind down around the firepit; nights end under more stars than you can count.
